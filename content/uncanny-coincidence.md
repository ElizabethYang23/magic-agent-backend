---
title: Uncanny Coincidence
difficulty: intermediate
props: [cards]
tags: [cards, coincidence, new-deck-order, spectator-selection]
source: "Unknown"
---
Effect: A deck is shown in new-deck order before a spectator freely selects a card. After several cuts and the spectator reinserts the card into the deck, the cards are spread face up to reveal that the chosen card has impossibly returned to its exact original position in the sequence.

Method summary: The routine uses a deck in new-deck order, a secret cut when the card is removed, a crimp to restore the deck's orientation after apparent random cuts, and Bill Simon's cut-around move to secretly return the selected card to its original location before the final revelation.
